## 6. AML & CNB Compliance 2026: Actionable Checklist

### Mandatory Compliance Checklist (CZ Emitent <5M EUR)

✓ **Know Your Customer (KYC)**
- Identity verification (ID card scan, proof of address)
- Beneficial ownership for corporate emitents (% stake >25%)
- Source of funds documentation (for investors >10k EUR single ticket)

✓ **Anti-Money Laundering (AML)**
- Transaction monitoring (flag >50k CZK suspicious patterns)
- Sanctions list cross-check (OFAC, ESMA blacklist)
- Quarterly reporting to CNB (if applicable; ECSP phase-in expected)

✓ **Product Offering Compliance**
- Prospekt per CZ Finance law (prospekt-lite if <1M EUR emission)
- Risk disclosure (clear language, jargon-free)
- Historic returns (if claiming track record, documented evidence required)

✓ **Data Protection (GDPR)**
- Privacy policy (explicit investor consent)
- Data retention limits (investor data: delete after 10Y if no activity)
- Breach notification protocol (<72 hours to supervisory authority)

✓ **Reporting & Audit**
- Annual financial reports (if emitent operates in CZ)
- External audit (mandatory if balance sheet >1M EUR)
- Investor communications (quarterly/annual updates required by law)

✓ **Platform-Level (OneFlow responsibility)**
- Payment services regulation (PSD3 2024, payment token handling)
- Cybersecurity (encryption, 2FA, penetration testing)
- Operational resilience (backup systems, 24-7 monitoring)
