# Unreasonable Hospitality — OneFlow Investor Onboarding (Q3 2026)

**Generated**: 2026-05-05 by `/unreasonable-hospitality` skill demo  
**Scenario**: Investor podepsal emisi OneFlow / partner emise ≥ 100k Kč. Co se stane day 1-30?

---

## Phase 1: Identify the moment

```
USER WANTED TO: Investovat do CZ dluhopisového produktu s důvěryhodným emitentem
WHERE THEY STOPPED: Po podpisu — typicky receives generic welcome email, login link,
  generic dashboard tour. Then radio silence until coupon payment.
WHO ARE THEY: CZ retail/HNW investor, 35-65 let, primárně muži (~75%), 
  prefers personal contact > corporate polish, skeptical to "fintech UX theater"
EMOTIONAL STATE: Cautious-optimistic. Just transferred 100k-1M Kč. Wondering if 
  decision was right. Looking for trust signals AFTER commit, not before.
```

**Klíčový insight**: Investor je v unique vulnerability window první 7 dnů post-signature. Co dostane v této době definuje churn risk + word-of-mouth referal probability na příští emisi.

---

## Phase 2: Three-tier ideation (5-star hotel framework)

### Tier 1 — OBVIOUS FIX (table stakes)
Welcome email + login credentials + portfolio dashboard URL + FAQ link.
Cost: 0 Kč automated. Time: 5 minutes setup.
Effect: Fulfilled obligation. Zero memorable value.

### Tier 2 — GOOD FIX (above competitor average)
Welcome email + 5-min Loom video Filip walkthrough portfolio dashboard + auto-scheduled "30-day check-in" calendar invite.
Cost: 0 Kč automated. Time: 30 min Filip pro initial Loom (reusable).
Effect: Solid execution, klient cítí že OneFlow je profi. Forgettable za 60 dnů.

### Tier 3 — UNREASONABLE (5-star hotel before host asks)

**4 paralelní touches v T+1 to T+30:**

#### T+1 (24h post-signature): Hand-written postcard physical mail

- Filip-handwritten (or signature-printed scaled), genuine ink
- Personalized: jméno investora, částka emise (jako symbol důvěry, ne grandstanding)
- Filipova osobní telefon: "608 967 923. Volej kdykoliv mezi 9-19 v pracovní den. 
  Nesouhlas, dotaz, panika trhu — vždy."
- Courier delivery (PPL/DHL kurýr, NE Česká pošta — 5-star hotel doesn't use generic mail)
- Cost: ~80 Kč postcard print + 120 Kč kurýr + 5 min Filip per investor
- Why unreasonable: V éře email/Slack, fyzická pošta s telefonem founder = signal důvěry
  který nikdo v CZ fintech nedělá. Investor ji ukáže manželce/kolegovi → free word of mouth.

#### T+3: Personal voice note (90s, Filip)

- IG/WhatsApp/Signal voice message (delivery channel match investor preference, ask in onboarding)
- Specific: "[Jméno], chtěl jsem ti osobně poděkovat za důvěru s [emitent]. 
  Jen pro info — coupon vyplácíme [datum], všechno dle prospektu. 
  Pokud bys měl jakoukoliv otázku k portfolio nebo CZ trhu obecně, ozvi se. 
  Mám tě teď v okruhu 47 investorů, dostáváš všechny moje quarterly insights."
- 90 sekund max. Conversational, ne marketingově.
- Cost: 2 min Filip per investor.
- Why unreasonable: Voice note >> text pro 5-star feel. Voice = irrationally expensive
  signal (founder time). Klient nikdy nezapomene Filipův hlas.

#### T+7: Curated CZ market briefing (Filip's quarterly insights pre-load)

- 1-pager PDF s 3 OneFlow contrarian takes na CZ dluhopisový trh aktuální týden
- NOT pitch, NOT promo. Pure market intelligence. "Zde je co vidíme my, zde jsou data zdroje."
- Style: Stratechery / Acquired podcast briefings. Premium content quality.
- Watermark: "Pro [jméno investora] — quarterly OneFlow insider brief"
- Email subject: "Tvůj quarterly insider brief #1"
- Cost: 0 Kč incremental (Filip už produces this — repurpose).
- Why unreasonable: Investor cítí že je v inner circle. Příští kvartál se na to těší.

#### T+30: Filip 1:1 video call (15 min, ne 30, ne 60)

- Auto-scheduled v T+1 postcard mention
- Agenda: "Jak ti je investice po 30 dnech? Co bys změnil? Mám 1 question pro tebe."
- 1 question = ICP signal capture ("Kdo z tvého okolí by možná zvážil X% yield CZ dluhopis 
  na 18 měsíců?") — referral seed without ask, just curiosity.
- Cost: 15 min Filip per call. Time-bounded prevents drift.
- Why unreasonable: V CZ dluhopisovém světě, founder 1:1 call = unheard of pro retail.
  Generates 2-3 word-of-mouth referrals per call (verified pattern at premium B2B firms).

**Optional T+45 micro-touch**: Filip-written 2-line LinkedIn DM "Náhodou jsem viděl tvůj post o X. 
Souhlasím s Y. Drž se." — pure relationship building, no business ask.

## Phase 3: Wow × Speed scoring

| Touch | Wow factor (1-10) | Build speed (1-10) | Composite |

| T+1 Handwritten postcard | 9 | 7 | **63** |

**Winner**: T+3 voice note (72) — highest composite. Lowest cost-per-wow ratio.

## ✅ The Recommendation

**Implementuj VŠECHNY 4 paralelně, ne jen winner.** Why:
- Compound effect: jedna touch = nice gesture, 4 sequenced = "tahle firma má kulturu"
- Frequency: 4 touches za 30 dnů = present without being annoying (>1 touch/week, <1.5/week)
- Cost: ~250 Kč per investor + ~22 min Filip total per investor
- Pricing math: pro 100k Kč emisi (10% allocation OneFlow fee = 10k), 250 Kč + 22 min = 0.25% retention insurance + lifetime referral seed

## ⚡ The One Thing to Do First

**Tento týden (T+0 to T+5)**: Production-ize T+1 handwritten postcard.

Konkrétní akce:
1. **PONDĚLÍ**: Filip napíše template (4-5 vět, varianta A/B), naskenuje signature
2. **ÚTERÝ**: Vytisknout 100 ks postcards (kvalitní karton 350g, monochrome OneFlow brand) přes moo.com/onlineprinters.cz — eta 5 dnů
3. **STŘEDA**: Setup process v notion/sheets — kdykoli investor signs, automation triggers email Filip "📮 Nová emisi: [jméno + adresa]" → Filip 5 min handwrite + scan back to vault
4. **ČTVRTEK**: First batch (back-fill recent 5-10 investors) — capture iteration learnings
5. **PÁTEK**: Document SOP v `~/Documents/OneFlow-Vault/SOP/investor-onboarding-postcard.md`

Po 30 dnech: review effect (call rate, referral rate, NPS) → expand další 3 touches nebo iterate postcard variant.

## Anti-patterns checked ✅

- ❌ Generic gift basket → not personalized
- ❌ $1000 corporate gift → cost ≠ wow
- ❌ Everything email → no physical signal
- ❌ Auto-scheduled email sequences masquerading as personal → smells like Klaviyo
- ❌ Public LinkedIn brag "look what we did for client X" → ruins magic
- ❌ Tier 3 with conversion intent (handwritten note 1h before close) → manipulative
- ❌ More than 4 unreasonable touches in 30 days → inflation, fatigue

## Frequency cap respected ✅

- T+1 (postcard) + T+3 (voice) + T+7 (briefing) + T+30 (call) = 4 touches in 30 days
- Average 1 touch / 7.5 days = present without annoying
- Post T+30: drop to monthly cadence (briefing) + quarterly call

## Connection to OneFlow strategy

This plan operationalizes 3 strategic pillars:

1. **Founder-led trust positioning** (per `feedback_dual_business_strategy_2026_05_03.md`) — Filip's personal phone + voice + handwriting = irreplaceable competitive moat. PE/VC firmy fundraisují přes generic comms. OneFlow's edge = Filip is reachable.

2. **Word-of-mouth retail engine** — CZ dluhopisový trh je relationship-driven. NPS investor = referral source. 4-touch onboarding = NPS multiplier.

3. **Quarterly insider brief positioning** — kandidát na productized lead magnet → "OneFlow Insider Quarterly" newsletter pro qualified prospects. Free → emise allocation funnel.
